# Cyclo-Help
Cyclo is a Discord Bot, that allows you to login to your Fortnite account and access multiple commands!

# Features
* Buy/Gift from the item shop
* Get your Account Name/ID
* Get your V-Bucks and the platforms of them
* Claim your Daily STW Reward
* Show your Locker
* Change Gift Message
* Change Gift Box
* Change status of receving gifts
* Buy the Battle Pass/Battle Bundle
* Change V-Bucks Platform
* Show your Fishing Book
* Checks Validity of Epic Games Code
* Show your Favorite Islands
* Add a Favorite Island
* Get friends list
* Add/Remove friends
* Change your Homebase Name
* Show your Party Hub Avatar
* Show your Gift History
* Get the current BR/STW shop
* And tons of more features that get added frequently!
* Also tons of General, and Moderation commands!

# Fortnite Commands
The standard prefix for Cyclo is ` + `

# Account Commands

## Login ## 
Command: `+login` \
Aliases: `+i`, `+signin` \
Logs into your Epic Games account using Device Code

## Logout ##
Command: `+logout` \
Aliases: `+o`, `+signout` \
Logs out of your Epic Games account 

## Delete Saved Account ##
Command: `+deletesaved` \
Aliases: `+del`, `+removesaved`, `+deletesavedlogin` \
Deletes your saved Fortnite account from the bot's database

## Claim 2FA Reward ##
Command: `+2fa` \
Aliases: `+mfa`, `+boogie` \
Claims the Boogie Down emote on your account. This is needed to be able to gift.\
You need to have to 2FA enabled on Epic Games first
